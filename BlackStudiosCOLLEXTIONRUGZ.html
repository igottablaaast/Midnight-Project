ABOUT

COLLEXTIONRUGZ is a black-owned business dedicated to creating quality handmade plush and cozy 2.5ft custom rugs. All rugs come with a standard pink felt backing. For cleaning, do not machine wash. Hand wash only the front of rugs with water, rag, and soap. Vaccum or lint roll to get rid of dust and extra fuzz.

Materials Used

  • Soft Acrylic Yarn
  • Pink Premium Felt Backing (Standard)
  • Other Color Premium Felt Backing (Upon Request)
  • Gray Non-Slip Backing (Upon Request)
