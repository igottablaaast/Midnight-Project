Welcome to COOK IT

Our Mission

This innovative application enables users to explore and experience diverse cultures from around the world, all from the comfort of their own kitchen.
By selecting a country, users gain access to a curated catalog of popular recipes, allowing them to immerse themselves in the culinary traditions of various
regions. Our platform promotes cultural appreciation and culinary exploration through an intuitive and engaging interface.
This app lets the user pick a continent and generates a catalog of popular recipes.

How it Works
  1. Select your Continent via drop down list
  2. Select a meat or vegetable option
  3. Choose a recipe
  4. Follow the recipe and enjoy
